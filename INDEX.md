## 🔄 Development Workflow

### To Make Changes:

1. **Edit a file** in AILifeOS/
2. **Build** (Cmd+B)
3. **Run** (Cmd+R)
4. **Test** in simulator
5. **Verify** no errors

### To Add a Feature:

1. **Create new View** (e.g., NewFeatureView.swift)
2. **Add to ContentView** tabs
3. **Connect to TransactionModel**
4. **Test** thoroughly
5. **Update docs**

### To Deploy:

1. **Complete testing** (TESTING_GUIDE.md)
2. **Add app icon** (assets)
3. **Archive in Xcode**
4. **Upload to TestFlight**
5. **Get feedback**
